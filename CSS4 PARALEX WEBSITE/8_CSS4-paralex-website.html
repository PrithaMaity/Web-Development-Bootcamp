PARA GLIDING

Paragliding is an exciting air sport that allows people to glide through the sky using a lightweight, fabric wing. The pilot takes off from a hill or mountain by running into the wind, and once airborne, they can soar for long distances using air currents. Paragliding offers a unique way to experience flight without an engine, giving a peaceful yet thrilling view of landscapes from above.

This adventure sport is popular in many countries and is enjoyed by both beginners and experienced flyers. It not only gives an adrenaline rush but also builds confidence and connects people with nature. SURFING

Surfing is a water sport where a person rides on the forward part of a moving wave, usually on a surfboard. It began as a cultural tradition in Polynesia and became popular around the world, especially in coastal areas with strong ocean waves. Surfers paddle out into the sea, wait for a suitable wave, and then ride it back toward the shore, balancing on their board with skill and precision.
